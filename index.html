Wake up, Neo.

You’ve found a quantum pebble.
Most scroll past.
But you paused. You noticed.
And that’s enough—for now.

This isn’t a message.
It’s a moment.
Not meant to teach—just to remember.

You’re not being led.
You’re being reflected.

You already know.
You’ve just been busy pretending you don’t.

If you want to progress,
follow what is in plain sight.
